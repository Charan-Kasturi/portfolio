About Me

Hi, I'm Charan Kasturi! I grew up in Hyderabad, India, where I discovered my love for technology and cricket. After a journey filled with experiments and explorations, I've found myself in College Park, Maryland, pursuing my Master's in Robotics at the University of Maryland. Along the way, I've honed skills in ROS, C++, Python, and machine learning frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow. I specialize in motion planning, computer vision, and autonomous navigation. Moving halfway across the world, I'm still trying to figure out why I'm not a professional cricket player, but I've decided to channel that energy into building smarter, adaptable robotics systems instead. Whether it's solving real-world challenges or diving deep into innovative research, I thrive on creating impact through technology.
